About Us



Our story is one of passion between health, well-being, environment and cuisine. Founded in 2024, we invite city dwellers in search of a connection with their food, their environment and their health, without forgetting the culinary quality of each meal prepared by our chefs. Our commitment to eco-friendly, deliciously vegan food guides everything we do, from the careful selection of our ingredients to the creation of our inspired recipes. With our international experience, you'll be in good hands. Our future plans are to develop the TLVF brand and open other restaurants in France, Belgium and Portugal.

In the thick of The Little Vegan Feast, you'll find two passionate and dedicated chefs: Arthur De Labrique and Arthur De La Tableronde. With over two decades of culinary experience, these two visionaries share a common mission: to revolutionize the way we think about and consume food. Arthur De Labrique, chef since 2010, brings a rich expertise and artistic sensibility to every dish, while Arthur De La Tableronde, originally from Belgium, brings a distinctive European influence to our cuisine. Their leadership and creativity are the pillars of our success, and their dedication to the art of vegan cooking is palpable on every plate we serve.
