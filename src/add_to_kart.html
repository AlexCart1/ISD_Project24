Description: Discover our Vegan Gourmet Burger, a true symphony of flavors and textures, carefully concocted to delight your taste buds. At The little garden, we are proud to offer you a 100% vegan burger, combining taste pleasure and respect for the planet. Our homemade plant-based patty, the heart of this burger, is prepared with chickpeas, black beans and quinoa. Seasoned with a subtle blend of spices, it offers a texture that is both crunchy on the outside and tender on the inside. Each bite reveals a richness of flavors, perfectly balanced. Soft, light and lightly toasted for an authentic taste, our vegan brioche bread is made without animal products. It harmoniously envelops our vegetable pancake, bringing a touch of sweetness to each bite.
