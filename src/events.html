Events

Welcome to the events page of our vegan, gluten-free, and organic restaurant! Our internationally renowned chefs, who are also stakeholders, invite you to join a variety of engaging activities. Enjoy insightful lectures on healthy eating, where you’ll learn tips and tricks for maintaining a balanced diet. Additionally, participate in our weekly cooking workshops, where our chefs will guide you through the preparation of delicious, plant-based, and gluten-free dishes. Check our regularly updated calendar for specific dates and times. We look forward to sharing our passion for healthy and sustainable cuisine with you!
